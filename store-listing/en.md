## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Dream Weaver creates personalized bedtime stories powered by AI, turning every night into a magical adventure tailored to your child.

**✨ How It Works**
Choose a theme, pick a story length, add your children's names — and Dream Weaver crafts a unique bedtime tale just for them. Every story is different, every night is special.

**🌙 7 Story Themes**
• Magic & Fantasy — enchanted worlds and magical creatures
• Nature & Animals — talking animals and nature spirits
• Wisdom & Mythology — ancient tales and philosophical journeys
• Emotions & Friendship — relatable social situations and emotional growth
• Moral Dilemmas — thought-provoking stories with no easy answers
• Modern Adventures — tech, school, and real-world challenges
• Daily Surprise — the AI picks the perfect theme for tonight

**📖 3 Story Lengths**
• Goodnight Kiss (~500 words) — quick and sweet
• Bedtime Story (~1,500 words) — the classic length
• Full Adventure (~2,500 words) — epic tales in chapters

**🎭 Interactive Stories**
Choose-your-own-adventure mode! Your child makes decisions that change the story. Two paths at every crossroad — every reading is unique.

**🎵 Premium Features**
• Premium AI narration voices (warm, natural, child-friendly)
• Ambient background music (piano, nature sounds, lullabies)
• Bedtime routine mode (music → story → dream bridge → lights out)
• Parent dashboard with listening insights
• Cloud sync across all your devices
• Unlimited story creation

**👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Built for Families**
• Add multiple child profiles with names and ages
• Stories adapt to age-appropriate language and complexity
• Safe, ad-free experience — no tracking, no ads, ever
• Works in 6 languages: English, Hebrew, Spanish, French, Arabic, Japanese
• Sleep timer that gently fades everything to silence
• Export and share your favorite stories

**🔒 Privacy First**
No ads. No tracking. Child data stays on your device. Read our privacy policy for full details.
