Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Dream Weaver ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered bedtime story generator for children. Your privacy and your children's privacy are our top priority.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Child profiles — First name and age only. These are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your private cloud account.
  • Account data — If you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email, and profile photo from Google. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other data.
  • Story preferences — Theme, length, and language choices used to generate stories.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage analytics — Story count, listening duration, and theme preferences. This data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • Crash reports — If enabled, anonymous crash data may be collected by the platform (Google Play).

1.3 Information we do NOT collect

  • Location data
  • Device contacts or photos
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history

2. How We Use Information

  • To generate personalized bedtime stories using AI (Anthropic Claude)
  • To provide text-to-speech narration (ElevenLabs, when premium voice is enabled)
  • To sync your stories and favorites across devices (Firebase, when signed in)
  • To process in-app purchases (Google Play Billing / RevenueCat)

3. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude API) Story text generation
ElevenLabs Premium voice narration
Firebase (Google) Authentication & cloud sync
RevenueCat Subscription management

Story text is sent to Anthropic's API for generation. No child names are sent unless you include them in a child profile — and even then, only the first name. We never send ages or other personal data to the AI.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Dream Weaver is designed for use by parents and guardians with their children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
  • The app is intended to be set up and supervised by a parent or guardian.
  • Child profile data (first name, age) is controlled by the parent and stored locally unless cloud sync is explicitly enabled.
  • We do not serve advertisements of any kind.
  • We do not share children's data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • If you enable cloud sync (sign in), data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) under your private account.
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

6. Your Rights

  • Delete — You can delete all data at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling.
  • Export — Stories can be exported as text files from within the app.
  • Opt out of cloud sync — Simply don't sign in. The app works fully offline.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
